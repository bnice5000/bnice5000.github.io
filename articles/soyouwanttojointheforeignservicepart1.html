So You Want to Join the Foreign Service, Part 1

Why do I want to join the foreign service?1

I just put in my application to be a Foreign Service Information Management Specialist. I am really excited. I have always wanted to join the foreign service, but I was not interested in the generalist job activities. The Information Management Specialist matched my interest but you needed a degree in computer science or one of the approved certifications.

I had waffled on it for years. I knew I could get one of the certifications that was required for the foreign service. I did not need a certification for my current or any of my previous jobs, so it was never a priority. This year was a bit of a roller coaster. My brother passed away and my wife is expecting our first born. I decided it was now or never.

I signed up for the CISSP exam in October and passed! Step one complete. After passing it was just a waiting game until they opened the application process.

I put in the application today. I spent a good amount of time working on the essays. I just submitted my application. I am very hopeful that I will advance in the process. Fingers crossed!

  1. I am not sure I will publish this blog entry. I guess it depends on my success in the application process. So, if you are reading this, I have been hired. ↩
