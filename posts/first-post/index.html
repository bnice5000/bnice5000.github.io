What Is This Blog?#

Things I Am Not Sure About#

I am not sure what I will write in this blog. I have a couple ideas that I have been kicking around for a while. I am really inspired by a couple of other broad bloggers.1 I have several hobbies and a lot of interests. I would also like a place to write about some of the neat things I see when I am out and about.

Things I Am Sure About#

The one thing I am sure about is that I will not have a comment section. They either devolve into pits of spam or unacceptable discourse. I am going to point people to my mastodon account. I would rather people contact me over there. It is a better platform and it allows me to better moderate what is happening. Also, I check it substantially more frequently than I would a comment section.

What Things May Come#

As for topics, this site will probably hue pretty close to my interests. Technology, security, radio, travel, tea, movies, music, television, museums, and art are all things I am passionate about. I will probably try to write about several of those things and see if there is any interest. At the very least, this will give me a creative outlet to write. I have always admired essayist2 and I want to use this as a platform to try to expand my writing ability in that area. I may also write about books or podcasts. I am an avid consumer of both, however, I am not sure there is much interest.

  1. Kottke, Plurialistic, Daring Fireball and And now it’s all this are some of my favorites. I am leaning toward (at least initially) modeling my posts after Dr. Drang’s format of semi-regular posts. However, I really like Kottke’s Quick Links posts. I might try a mix of things and see what feels right. ↩︎

  2. I really enjoy essays by Pauline Kael, Erma Bombeck, Dave Barry, and Bill Bryson. I don’t want to copy their style but it is very likely to happen as you are always influenced (even when you try to avoid it) by your inspirations. ↩︎
