So You Want to Join the Foreign Service, Part 4

Date 22 Oct 2016 Tags Application

The Steps to join the Foreign Service

  1. Meet the requirements for the position (e.g. get my CISSP) Completed 2015-09-20
  2. Apply to the USAJobs posting Completed 2015-12-15
  3. Pass qualified evaluations panel Completed 2015-02-20
  4. Pass the oral assessment Completed 2015-05-12
  5. Get medical clearance Completed 2015-06-10
  6. Get security clearance
  7. Pass final suitability review panel
  8. Get placed on register
  9. Be selected off the register for an A100 class
  10. Success!

I appear to be in the throws of a long wait. While I submitted my information as promptly as I could, It seems as if my security package was misplaced. I turned everything that was required in on May 27th. I emailed the security clearance email address fortnightly to check on its progress. I was told everything was well and good. Finally after 60 days I became worried. I finally decided to get a little more assertive.

After several phone calls I was able to ascertain that even though I had submitted everything to the Department of State on May 27th, my package was not received by the Department of State Security Clearance Intake Unit until July 8th. So I would likely have to wait 60 days from July 8th for the process to actually start.

I have to say that this felt like a setback. I thought I might have a chance at the September class. It seems like that was not meant to be.
